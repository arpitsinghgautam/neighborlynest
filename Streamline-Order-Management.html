Streamlining Order Management

Step 1

Create a basic site on Google Sites and list out your items, ensuring to include essential information such as your products or services and contact number. This step establishes an initial online presence for your business, providing potential customers with key details.

Google Sites

Step 2

Create a profile on Google My Business and add your business to the map. This allows your business to appear in local search results and enhances its visibility. Additionally, link your website created on Google Sites to your Google My Business profile, enabling customers to easily access your website from the search results.

Google Business

Step 3

Go to Google Search Console and verify and submit your website there. This step helps Google understand your website's structure and content, improving its chances of appearing in relevant search results. By providing data to Google Search Console, you enhance your website's visibility and increase the likelihood of attracting organic traffic.

Google Search Console

Step 4

Once your online presence is established, you can start waiting for orders to come. Potential customers who find your business through Google search or Google Maps can contact you via the provided contact number or through the website. Ensure you have a streamlined order management process in place to efficiently track and fulfill orders, ensuring customer satisfaction and loyalty.
